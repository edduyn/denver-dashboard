## GOALS AND OBJECTIVES FOR NEXT REVIEW PERIOD

**Performance goals:**
Edduyn's Goals and Objectives for 2026 are as follows:

### 1. Financial Objectives - A/B and ELR
Meet or exceed the Denver Satellite financial objectives for 2026. Continued emphasis should be placed on your shop's Actual/Bought (A/B), and Effective Labor Rate (ELR) objectives.

**Tracking:** Dashboard metrics
**Key Metrics:** A/B Ratio (Target: 58.5%), ELR
**Status:** Tracked via daily/weekly reports

---

### 2. Net Promoter Score Program
Participate fully in the Net Promoter Score program, and achieve a recordable number of entries (goal remains 200 for the entire network). Your goal should be one per month.

**Tracking:** NET Promoter results folder
**Target:** 1 entry per month (12 per year)
**Network Goal:** 200 total entries
**Status:** Check nps_surveys table

---

### 3. Training Completion (100% On Time)
Ensure that you and your team complete all mandatory and technical training events in 2026 on time. This includes NEO/NETO checklists for new team members.

**Tracking:** Training Due Report
**Target:** 100% completion on time
**Includes:** Mandatory, technical, NEO/NETO checklists
**Status:** Check training table

---

### 4. Flat-Rate System Re-evaluation
Re-evaluate the performance and objectives of the flat-rate system you have in place in Denver, using hard data. Let's make sure it is successful before we start to roll it out to other shops.

**Tracking:** squawk_data.json (Hours Sold vs Hours Actual)
**Objective:** Success/Fail analysis using hard data
**Scope:** Denver flat-rate system evaluation
**Outcome:** Determine readiness for network rollout

---

### 5. Customer Experience - Communication
Manage your customers experience by ensuring prompt, consistent, and professional communication through improved availability, faster response times, and reliable phone coverage.

**Tracking:** Manual/Process
**Focus Areas:**
- Improved availability
- Faster response times
- Reliable phone coverage
**Standard:** Prompt, consistent, professional

---

### 6. Expense Budget Management
Manage your shop expenses so that you will stay within budgeted amounts.

**Tracking:** Financial reports vs Budget
**Target:** Stay within budgeted amounts
**Review:** Monthly expense tracking
**Status:** Check financial reports

---

### 7. FAA/Internal Audits - Zero Findings
Complete all FAA and internal audits with no findings.

**Tracking:** Manual
**Target:** Zero findings
**Scope:** All FAA and internal audits
**Status:** Audit results tracking

---

### 8. Monthly A/B Plan
Put together a plan to make sure to meet budget each month on your Actual/Bought metric.

**Tracking:** Dashboard
**Target:** Meet A/B budget monthly (58.5%)
**Objective:** Consistent monthly performance
**Status:** Monthly A/B tracking

---

### 9. Dealer Programs Tab - Exclusive Use
Following the new Dealer Program policies, use the Dealer Programs tab exclusively, and track customer reaction and success rates.

**Tracking:** Dealer Programs folder
**Requirement:** Exclusive use of Dealer Programs tab
**Metrics:** Customer reaction, success rates
**Policy:** New Dealer Program policies compliance

---

### 10. Vendor Dispute Communication (< 30 Days)
When you have a vendor dispute, be sure to communicate it to BZ within 30 days.

**Tracking:** WO Disputes folder
**Target:** Communicate to Brian Zitek (BZ) within 30 days
**Objective:** 100% on-time communication
**Status:** Check dispute tracking

---

### 11. Risk Area Identification
Identify some areas of risk within your Satellite that may help to reduce our overall risk assessment score.

**Tracking:** Strategic
**Objective:** Proactive risk identification
**Focus:** Safety, quality, compliance risk areas
**Outcome:** Reduce overall risk assessment score
