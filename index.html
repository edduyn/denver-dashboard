ℹ️ About WIP (tap to expand)

Work in Progress (WIP) smooths out billing peaks by accounting for labor and parts sold to work orders on a monthly basis.

  • Standard WIP: SDN, SDR, SHC @ $155/hr + 12% parts margin (2026 rate)
  • Special WIP: SDV installs @ $80/hr + 12% parts margin
  • Parts budget target: 23% overall margin on full billed WO (SDV target ~18%)
  • Scope: Only WOs opened in current month (or with additional hours sold this month)
  • Goal: Always be AHEAD — WIP accrued > actual billing = good
  • Special WIP deadline: Email to Matt 2 business days before month-end
